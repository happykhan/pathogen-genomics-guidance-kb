# Terms of Reference: Technical Working Group

## Name

Pathogen Genomics Data and Infrastructure Guidance Technical Working Group

## Purpose

To develop pathogen-agnostic best-practice guidance for the management of pathogen genomics data and the infrastructure needed for sustainable genomics surveillance.

## Objectives

- Consolidate existing community best practices.
- Define practical guidance across the data lifecycle.
- Identify reusable principles across pathogen and disease programmes.
- Produce implementation-oriented resources for countries and laboratories.
- Support alignment across WHO, CPG, CGPS, Fiocruz, PHA4GE, and related partners.

## Proposed Workstreams

- data lifecycle and governance
- QC and analytical principles
- storage, backup, archiving, and retention
- compute infrastructure and workflow systems
- metadata and data standards
- workforce, sustainability, and procurement
- case studies and implementation tools

## Outputs

- guidance outline
- modular guidance chapters
- country readiness checklist
- infrastructure requirements checklist
- data lifecycle checklist
- implementation vignettes
- source/provenance index

## Membership

The group should include expertise in public health genomics, bioinformatics, data management, laboratory operations, central IT, cybersecurity, cloud/HPC infrastructure, standards, AMR, One Health, and country implementation.

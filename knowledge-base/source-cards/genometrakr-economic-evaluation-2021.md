- PMID: 34614029.
- DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0258262.

## Status

`candidate extracted summary`

## Relevance

This is a priority source for the economic value case for pathogen genomics in food safety. The abstract reports that the FDA GenomeTrakr WGS Network was assessed through theoretical, empirical, and cost-benefit analyses. It links added WGS isolates in NCBI Pathogen Detection to reductions in outbreak illnesses for pilot pathogens and estimates large annual health benefits relative to public-health-agency investment.

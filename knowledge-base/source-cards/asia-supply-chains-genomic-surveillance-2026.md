## Extracted Notes

- The abstract reports procurement and supply-chain challenges across South and Southeast Asia for pathogen genomics.
- Reported barriers include high equipment and consumable costs, long equipment purchasing lead times, long reagent resupply times, customs clearance, variable import procedures, taxes, and duties.
- The paper argues for coordinated national and regional efforts to improve supply-chain systems for timely disease detection and response.

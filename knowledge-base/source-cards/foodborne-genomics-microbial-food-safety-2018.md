## Extracted Notes

- The review describes WGS use across foodborne pathogens including Salmonella, Escherichia coli, Listeria, Campylobacter, and Vibrio.
- It identifies applications including outbreak detection and characterisation, source tracking, root-cause investigation, virulence/pathogenicity profiling, AMR monitoring, and microbiology quality assurance.
- It frames public sharing and integrated networks such as GenomeTrakr/PulseNet as central to food-safety and public-health use.

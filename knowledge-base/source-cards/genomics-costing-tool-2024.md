## Relevance

This source describes a genomics costing tool developed through a multi-agency collaboration involving APHL, FIND, The Global Fund, UKHSA, and WHO. The abstract states that the tool supports financial planning and budgeting for sequencing and bioinformatics activities, including infrastructure, consumables, human resources, facilities, and quality management.

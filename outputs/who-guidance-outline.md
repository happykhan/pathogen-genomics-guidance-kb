# WHO Guidance Outline: Pathogen Genomics Data and Infrastructure

## Working Title

Best Practices and Guiding Principles for Pathogen Genomics Data Management and Bioinformatics Infrastructure

## Draft Structure

1. Executive summary
2. Purpose, scope, and intended audience
3. Principles for sustainable pathogen genomics data systems
4. Data lifecycle management
5. Quality control across the lifecycle
6. Metadata, identifiers, and data standards
7. Storage, backup, archiving, and retention
8. Compute infrastructure and operating models
9. Workflow management, reproducibility, and provenance
10. Access control, governance, and security
11. General analytical principles
12. Workforce, sustainability, and procurement
13. Implementation maturity levels
14. Country/laboratory readiness assessment
15. Case studies and implementation vignettes
16. Checklists and templates

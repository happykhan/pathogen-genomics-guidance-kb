## Notes

- WHO and ECDC sources are likely the best next sources for formal framing, surveillance objectives, data-sharing principles, and cross-border use.
- APHL and CDC/APHL sources are likely the best next sources for quality management and practical lab implementation.
- UKHSA and Australian sources are likely useful for strategy, governance, national coordination, and maturity/capability framing.
- Candidate sources should be extracted before being cited in `docs/`.
